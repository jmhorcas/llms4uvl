Each pizza needs a Base, exactly one type of Sauce (Tomato or Creme Fraiche), and may contain one or multiple types of Cheese (Mozzarella, Cheddar). 
Cheddar cannot be selected with Creme Fraiche. 
The price of a feature is atached as additional information: Base 2.0, Tomato 0.8, Creme Fraiche 1.1, Mozarella 0.4, Cheddar 0.6, Mushrooms 0.5, Onion 0.3, Olive 0.3, Pineapple 0.8. 
Between one and three Toppings can be selected (Mushrooms, Onion, Olive, Pineapple). 
Multiple delivery addresses ([1..10]) can be configured (Street and City). 
The overall price needs to be smaller than 5. 
The Size of the pizza is an integer and the Street and City are strings. 
The Size needs to be between 20 and 60. 
The delivery location needs to be in the same City as the pizza place ('Omashu') and the length of the Street may be at most 100.
