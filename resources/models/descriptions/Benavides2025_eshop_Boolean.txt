A fictitious eShop product line. 
eShop has two mandatory and three optional child features.
Any configuration of an eShop has to have a Catalogue and a Security option.
A configuration of an eShop can optionally have SEO, Payment, and Platform support. 
Exactly one Security option (Standard or High) and exactly one Payment option can be selected. 
The Payment of the eShop must be either Bank Transfer or Credit card (but not both in the same configuration).
Whenever Platform is selected, Mobile App, Browser or any combination thereof including at least one of these two features can be selected.
An eShop including Credit card must include High security support.
The Bank Transfer feature cannot be combined with a Mobile App, i.e., these two features are incompatible.
In addition there are some attributes that provide information on the respective features.
We have a number attribute (price), a Boolean attribute (SEPA), and a string attribute (URL):
High 100, Standard 50, Bank Transfer 10 and SEPA, Credit Card 20, and Browser 'www.uvleshop.org'.
